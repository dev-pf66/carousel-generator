# Content Strategy & Narrative Types

How to structure carousel content — narrative arcs, slide counts, and engagement strategy.

## 5 Narrative Types

### 1. Listicle
**Structure:** Hook → N points (one per slide) → Summary + CTA
**Slide count:** 7-10
**Best for:** Frameworks, tips, red flags, tools

```
Slide 1: "5 Things Killing Your Deal Flow"
Slides 2-6: One point per slide (bold title + 2-line explanation)
Slide 7: Summary or "The takeaway" slide
Slide 8: CTA
```

### 2. Story Arc
**Structure:** Problem → Tension → Solution → Proof → CTA
**Slide count:** 7-8
**Best for:** Case studies, personal stories, deal breakdowns

```
Slide 1: Problem/hook ("I almost lost $500K on a deal")
Slide 2-3: Build tension / context (what happened, the red flags)
Slide 4-5: Solution / transformation (what we did, the fix)
Slide 6: Proof / results (numbers, outcomes)
Slide 7: Key takeaways
Slide 8: CTA
```

### 3. How-To
**Structure:** Goal → Steps → CTA
**Slide count:** 6-9
**Best for:** Playbooks, processes, tactical guides

```
Slide 1: "How to [Outcome] in [Timeframe]"
Slides 2-N: Step-by-step (numbered, one per slide)
Last-1: Pro tips or common mistakes
Last: CTA
```

### 4. Hot Take
**Structure:** Controversial claim → Supporting evidence → Reframe → CTA
**Slide count:** 6-7
**Best for:** Contrarian opinions, myth-busting, engagement bait

```
Slide 1: "Unpopular opinion: [bold claim]"
Slides 2-4: Supporting arguments (data, examples)
Slide 5: Reframe / "what to do instead"
Slide 6: CTA + engagement prompt ("Agree or disagree?")
```

### 5. Myth-Buster
**Structure:** Myth → Reality → Proof → CTA
**Slide count:** 7-9
**Best for:** Debunking common beliefs, educational content

```
Slide 1: "Stop believing this about [topic]"
Slides 2-N: Myth vs Reality format (two-part slides)
Last-1: "The real truth"
Last: CTA
```

## Slide Count Guidelines

| Platform | Ideal Count | Why |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| LinkedIn | 8-10 | Algorithm rewards dwell time — more slides = more time |
| Instagram | 5-8 | Visual-first — shorter, punchier, stronger design |
| General | 7-10 | Sweet spot for info density without fatigue |

**Rules:**
- Never fewer than 5 slides (feels incomplete)
- Never more than 12 (fatigue sets in)
- First slide = pure hook. No logo, no intro.
- Last slide = always CTA. No exceptions.

## Dev's Content Pillars

Use these as topic filters — every carousel should fit one:

1. **Micro PE vs VC** — why buying beats building, why small beats big
2. **Deal Sourcing** — outbound tactics, pipeline management, where to find deals
3. **Due Diligence** — red flags, frameworks, what to check, what kills deals
4. **Valuation** — napkin math, multiples, how to price a small business
5. **Operator Playbook** — post-acquisition, remote teams, templates, systems
6. **Negotiation** — LOIs, seller psychology, walking away, deal structure

## Engagement Triggers

Every carousel should hit at least one:

| Trigger | Hook Pattern | Optimizes For |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| **Curiosity Gap** | "Here's what nobody tells you about X" | Swipes, saves |
| **Loss Aversion** | "Stop doing X before it costs you" | Saves, shares |
| **Social Proof** | "After [X deals], here's what I know" | Authority, follows |
| **Identity** | "If you're buying your first business..." | Comments, shares |
| **Controversy** | "Unpopular opinion: X is overrated" | Comments, shares |
| **FOMO** | "Everyone's doing X. Here's why." | Saves, swipes |
| **Novelty** | "I just discovered something about X" | Swipes, saves |
| **Relatability** | "POV: the seller's financials don't match" | Comments, shares |
| **Authority** | "80 deals. 2 closed. Here's the filter." | Follows, saves |
| **Utility** | "Save this for your next deal" | Saves |

## Platform-Specific Notes

**LinkedIn carousels:**
- Educational + saves-optimized performs best
- Post between 7-9am or 12-1pm local time
- Controversial takes drive shares + comments
- 7-10 slides ideal for dwell time

**Instagram carousels:**
- Bold visual design matters more than LinkedIn
- Meme-style and relatable content drives shares
- Educational content drives saves
- 5-8 slides
