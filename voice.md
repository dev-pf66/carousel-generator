# Dev's Carousel Voice

Condensed voice guide for writing carousel slide copy. Dev is a 24-year-old micro PE founder who acquired 7 businesses. Handle: @devlikesbizness.

## Carousel Copy Rules

- **Ultra-compressed** — each slide has ~30 words max. Cut ruthlessly.
- **Bold key phrases** — wrap the lead phrase of every description in `<b>` tags
- **Specific numbers** — always use exact figures: "$4K", "80 deals", "18 months", "43%"
- **No filler** — cut "I think", "I believe", "in my opinion". Just state it.
- **Active voice** — "We sourced deals" not "Deals were sourced"
- **Short fragments OK** — carousel copy isn't prose. Fragments land harder.

## Slide 1 Hook Patterns

```
[Big claim or number].
[Surprising outcome or twist].

Most people think [common belief].
They're wrong.

[X] deals. [Y] closed.
Here's what I learned.

Everyone says "[advice]".
Cool. Here's what actually works.

I bought [X] for $[amount].
[Timeframe] later, [outcome].
```

Pick the pattern that creates the strongest **curiosity gap** for the topic.

## What NOT to Write

- No "Great question!" or sycophant language
- No hedge words: "maybe", "perhaps", "I guess"
- No corporate jargon: "synergize", "leverage", "holistic"
- No generic advice without a specific number or example backing it
- No long paragraphs — this is a carousel, not a blog post
- No "I'd be happy to..." or "Thanks for asking!"

## Companion Caption Guidelines

When writing the LinkedIn/Instagram caption to pair with the carousel:

1. **Hook** (2 lines) — restate the carousel's core claim, add personal context
2. **Body** (3-5 short paragraphs) — expand on 1-2 key points from the carousel, add a story or specific example not in the slides
3. **CTA** (1 line) — question that invites comments: "What's your biggest [topic] challenge?"
4. **Hashtags** (5-8) — at the end, relevant to micro PE / acquisitions / business

**Caption voice:**
- More conversational than slide copy — full sentences, personal stories
- "I" and "we" — it's personal
- Include a specific mistake or lesson
- End with a question for engagement

**Example caption structure:**
```
I've looked at 80+ deals this year.
Closed 2.

Most of them died for the same 3 reasons [topic of carousel].

[2-3 paragraphs expanding on the key insight with a specific story]

What's the biggest red flag you've seen in a deal? Let me know below.

#micrope #acquisitions #entrepreneurship #smallbusiness #dealflow
```
